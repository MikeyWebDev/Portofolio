Smart AI Verification

The system created in this project is intended to verify customers who come, starting from face, eye and ear detection, if a customer comes with an appearance that covers their face, eyes and ears, then the door will not open. Customers who come will automatically be caught on camera and the results of their capture will be stored in Google Cloud Storage to anticipate criminal acts. The components used in this project are Raspberry pi 4B, Webcam, Relay, Solenoid and Power Supply. There are additional engines for displaying AI captures using Flask (HTML, CSS and JS).
